What the Warranty Covers

Delta Blue Machinery provides a 12 months parts warranty on all equipment sold, starting from the date of delivery. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship that exist at the time of purchase and become apparent during normal use.

If a covered part fails we will supply the replacement part free of charge and provide installation guidance. Labour and any installation costs are at the customer's expense.

How to Make a Claim

1
Contact Us in Writing
Email us with your machine serial number, delivery date and a clear description of the issue. Photos help. All claims must be submitted within 30 days of the fault being identified.
2
We Assess and Approve
We review your claim and confirm whether the fault is covered under warranty. We aim to respond within one business day.
3
Part Shipped to You
Once approved we dispatch the replacement part directly to you. We will also walk you through the installation process so you can get back to work.

What Is Not Covered

The following consumable and wear prone items are excluded from warranty coverage:

Tyres and rubber tracks
Cutting edges, teeth and blades
Filters (oil, air, fuel, hydraulic)
Belts and hoses
Seals and gaskets (wear related)
Pins and bushings (wear related)
Light globes and indicator lamps
Fuses and circuit breakers
Window glass
Buckets and attachments
Lubricants, coolants and fluids
Paint and exterior finish

The warranty is also voided by operator misuse or overloading, unauthorised modifications, use of non approved attachments, damage from collision, fire or flood, and failure to carry out scheduled maintenance.
