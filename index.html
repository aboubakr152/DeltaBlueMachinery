Warranty Period & Coverage

Delta Blue Machinery provides a 12 month warranty on all equipment sold, commencing on the date of delivery. This warranty covers defects in materials or workmanship that exist at the time of purchase and become apparent during normal use of the machine.

All warranty claims must be submitted to Delta Blue Machinery in writing within 30 days of the issue being identified. No warranty work should commence without prior written approval from us. Claims submitted outside this timeframe may not be accepted.

How We Handle Repairs

1
Local Mechanic: We Reimburse
You engage a qualified local mechanic for the approved repair. Delta Blue Machinery reimburses reasonable labour costs up to $150 + GST per hour and covers approved replacement parts. Prior written approval is required, and the mechanic must hold relevant trade qualifications.
2
Return to Us for Repair
Alternatively, the machine may be returned to our facility. The customer is responsible for transport costs to and from our location. Once received, we carry out all approved repairs at no charge and return the machine to you.

What Is Covered

The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship from the manufacturing process, including structural components, hydraulic systems, drivetrain components and electrical systems, provided the machine has been operated and maintained in accordance with the supplied operator's manual.

Exclusions

The following consumable, wear-prone or operator-caused items are excluded from warranty coverage:

Tyres and rubber tracks
Cutting edges, teeth and blades
Filters (oil, air, fuel, hydraulic)
Belts and hoses
Seals and gaskets (wear-related)
Pins and bushings (wear-related)
Light globes and indicator lamps
Fuses and circuit breakers
Window glass
Buckets and attachments
Lubricants, coolants and fluids
Paint and exterior finish

The warranty is also voided by: operator misuse or overloading; unauthorised modifications; use of non-approved attachments; damage from collision, fire or flood; and failure to carry out scheduled maintenance.

Maintenance & Records

All machines require periodic maintenance as specified in the operator's manual. Failure to maintain the machine correctly may void all or part of the warranty. We strongly recommend retaining service records and receipts. Paint and surface finish are not covered. Regular cleaning and appropriate storage are recommended to preserve machine appearance.

Return & Refund Policy

New, unopened items in original packaging with intact serial numbers may be returned within 15 days of delivery, subject to prior notification. All returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee, with return freight at the customer's cost.

  • Items that have been opened, operated or used in any way
  • Items missing serial numbers, original packaging or accessories
  • Special or custom orders (at our discretion)
  • Items returned more than 15 days after delivery without prior notification

Limitation of Liability

Delta Blue Machinery's liability is limited to the repair or replacement of defective parts. We are not liable for indirect, consequential or economic losses including machine downtime, loss of productivity or replacement hire costs. Delta Blue Machinery does not authorise any person to extend or modify these warranty terms.

Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this warranty excludes, restricts or modifies any right or remedy under the Australian Consumer Law (Schedule 2 of the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 (Cth)). Our goods come with guarantees that cannot be excluded. You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage. You are also entitled to have goods repaired or replaced if they fail to be of acceptable quality.
